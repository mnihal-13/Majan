About Us

Your vision is our lifelong commitment

At Majaan Eye Care, we are dedicated to providing exceptional eye care services with a focus on precision, comfort, and innovation at the heart of Barka . Our team of experienced professionals offers personalized eye care solutions, from routine checkups to advanced treatments. Trust us to safeguard your eye health and enhance your quality of life.

  • Advanced technology for cataract, glaucoma & pediatric care.
  • Affordable, compassionate service for every age and need.

Mission

At Majan Eye Centre, we provide personalized, comprehensive eye care through advanced diagnostics, skilled professionals, and evidence-based practices, with a focus on patient satisfaction and safety.

Vision

To be a trusted leader in ophthalmic care across Oman, offering world-class eye treatments with integrity, innovation, and empathy. We'll ensure that every patient experiences the highest level of care.

Values

We prioritize trust, empathy, and human connection. Every patient is treated with respect and care, like family, offering not just solutions, but peace of mind and a future seen clearly.

Why Choose Us

Trusted by Thousands, Driven by Care

At Majan Eye Centre, we’re not just an eye hospital we’re a vision partner for life. From restoring clarity through cataract surgery to protecting sight with expert glaucoma care, from nurturing young vision in our pediatric clinic to managing delicate conditions like diabetic retinopathy, we offer more than serviceswe offer peace of mind. With YAG laser therapy and intravitreal injections, we bring international standards to your doorstep in Barka. For every eye, every age, and every need, we see you and we care.
