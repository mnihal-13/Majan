Our Main Services

Cataract Clinic

Our Cataract Clinic helps patients whose natural eye lens has become cloudy with age. We remove the cloudy lens and replace it with a clear artificial one, often restoring brighter vision within days. This routine procedure is done carefully and typically with minimal discomfort. The result is clearer sight and a renewed ability to enjoy daily activities with confidence.

Glaucoma Clinic

Our Glaucoma Clinic focuses on catching and managing glaucoma, a condition where fluid pressure inside the eye can damage vision over time. We regularly check your eye pressure and provide medication or gentle laser procedures to keep your optic nerve safe. With careful treatment, we help prevent further vision loss and preserve your sight.

Retina Clinic

Our Retina Clinic cares for conditions affecting the back of the eye (the retina) that are vital for vision. We treat issues like diabetic eye disease, macular degeneration, and retinal tears using advanced, gentle treatments. These methods aim to stop disease progression and protect your sight. We understand these conditions can be concerning, so we explain each step clearly and work to keep your vision as healthy as possible.

Paediatric Ophthalmology Clinic

Our Paediatric Ophthalmology Clinic is dedicated to children’s eye health. We diagnose and treat common childhood eye problems, such as crossed eyes (strabismus), lazy eye (amblyopia), or the need for glasses. Early intervention helps children see clearly and develop properly. We make eye exams child-friendly and explain everything kindly, so kids feel comfortable and parents feel reassured.

Oculoplastic Clinic

Our Oculoplastic Clinic focuses on eyelid and tear duct conditions. We perform procedures like eyelid lifts, tear duct repairs, or removal of eyelid growths, improving both comfort and appearance. These treatments can help if droopy eyelids are blocking your vision or if you have lid problems from injury. Our team takes care to ensure your eyes look and feel better while protecting your eyesight.

Contact Lens/Keratoconus Clinic

Our Contact Lens/Keratoconus Clinic fits the right lenses for your eyes, including special ones for keratoconus. We have a variety of contact lenses available, from soft to rigid, to give you the clearest vision possible. Using the correct lens can reduce or even eliminate your need for glasses. Our specialists make sure your lenses are comfortable and teach you how to care for them so you can see well in daily life.

Other Services

Traumatic Surgery

Our Traumatic Surgery service treats serious eye injuries from accidents or trauma. Our skilled surgeons quickly repair damage to the eye’s structures—such as cuts or foreign objects—to protect or restore vision. We give your eye the best chance to heal fully.

Intravitreal injection

Our Intravitreal Injection service provides tiny injections of medication directly into the back of the eye to treat serious conditions. This is used for diseases like wet macular degeneration or diabetic eye swelling, delivering medicine right where it’s needed.

Syringing and Probing

Our Syringing and Probing service clears blocked tear ducts, allowing tears to drain normally. A gentle flushing and thin probing open the tear pathways that may have been causing constant infections. This simple treatment helps your eyes stay comfortable

Chalazion Excision

Our Chalazion Excision procedure removes a chalazion, which is a small lump on the eyelid caused by a blocked oil gland. We make a tiny incision and remove the hardened material from the eyelid. This minor procedure heals quickly and relieves the pressure or irritation caused by the chalazion. Removing it helps keep your eyelid healthy and the eye comfortable.

Suture Removal

Our Suture Removal service gently takes out stitches after eye surgery once the eye has healed enough. We carefully remove any sutures so they don’t cause irritation or affect your vision. It’s a quick and usually painless step that helps your eye finish healing. After the stitches are removed, many patients feel immediate relief and improved comfort in their eye.

Low Vision Aids

Our Low Vision Aids service offers special devices to help people with limited vision make the most of their remaining sight. We provide items like magnifying glasses, telescopic lenses, and electronic readers that enlarge text or images. These aids make everyday tasks—like reading, cooking, or watching TV—much easier and safer. We help you choose the best aids.

Blepharoplasty

Our Blepharoplasty service is eyelid surgery to remove excess skin or fat from the upper or lower eyelids. Removing droopy eyelid tissue can improve your field of vision and give your eyes a more refreshed appearance. The procedure is done carefully to ensure safety and a natural result. Many patients notice immediate relief from eyelid heaviness.

Entropion Surgery

Our Entropion Surgery fixes an eyelid that has turned inward, causing eyelashes to rub against the eye. We gently reposition or tighten the eyelid so lashes point outward again. This protects your cornea from scratches and relieves irritation or tearing. After surgery, your eye feels much more comfortable and is better protected.

Pterygium Excision

Our Pterygium Excision service removes a pterygium, which is a benign growth on the white of the eye that can extend onto the clear cornea. This fleshy growth often comes from sun exposure and can cause redness, irritation, or vision problems if it grows. Removing it is a simple procedure that relieves discomfort.

Treating Retinal Holes & Diabetic Retinopathy

In our Retinal Holes & Diabetic Retinopathy service, we use lasers or injections to treat and stabilize retina problems. Small holes or tears in the retina can be sealed with laser therapy to prevent a detachment. For diabetic retinopathy (damage from high blood sugar), we use laser or medication injections to reduce swelling and bleeding in the eye. These treatments help save vision and reduce the risk of serious sight loss from these conditions.

Medical Retina & Diabetic Screening

Our Medical Retina & Diabetic Screening service offers thorough examinations of the retina for conditions like diabetic retinopathy, macular degeneration, and other eye diseases. We use special cameras and eye drops to get a clear view of the retina at the back of your eye. Early detection of any issues allows us to start treatment sooner, protecting your vision. We guide you through the process and explain results clearly.

Treating Glaucoma & After Cataract Complication

Our Glaucoma & Cataract Care service manages two related concerns to protect your vision. We control glaucoma by lowering eye pressure with medications or gentle procedures to prevent optic nerve damage. We also address any issues that can follow cataract surgery, such as inflammation or temporary vision changes, making sure your eye heals properly. Our team monitors you closely and provides support at every step.

Pleopatic and Orthoptic Treatment of Strabismus & Amblyopia

Our Strabismus & Amblyopia treatment uses special vision therapies and exercises to help children with crossed eyes (strabismus) or lazy eye (amblyopia). These fun, guided activities encourage the weaker eye to strengthen and both eyes to align better. Over time, therapy improves vision and depth perception, helping children see more clearly. We make the therapy engaging and supportive, so kids gain confidence as their vision improves.

Comprehensive Eye Check up for all Eye Diseases

Our Comprehensive Eye Check-up covers every aspect of eye health in one appointment. We test your vision, measure eye pressure, and examine the front and back of your eyes to look for any signs of disease. Catching problems early means we can treat them before they affect your vision. We explain each step clearly so you understand your eye health and how to keep it in good shape.

Meibomian Gland Probing (M.G.D treatment)

Our Meibomian Gland Probing treats dry eye caused by clogged oil glands in the eyelids (Meibomian Gland Dysfunction). We use a fine probe to gently open these blocked glands and restore normal oil flow in your tears. This improves tear quality and brings relief from dryness, burning or blurry vision. Many patients feel their eyes become much more comfortable after this quick.

Eye Exam of Neonates
( pt < 1 year)

Our Neonatal Eye Exam provides a gentle eye check for babies under 1 year old. We examine newborns’ eyes carefully to detect any early problems. Early detection means we can treat any issues quickly, giving your baby the best chance for healthy vision. We handle infants with special care, making the experience safe and stress-free for both baby and parents.

Conjuctival / Eyelid Cyst Removal

Our Conjunctival/Eyelid Cyst Removal service safely removes small, fluid-filled bumps on the surface of the eye or eyelid. These harmless cysts can cause irritation or slightly blur vision if they grow. We remove them with a quick, minor procedure so you feel comfortable. After removal, most patients experience immediate relief and can see clearly without the obstruction.
