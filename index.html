About Us

Skip to World of Difference with Expert Eye Care.

Majan Eye Centre is a premier ophthalmology hospital located in Barka, Sultanate of Oman, established to meet the growing demand for specialized eye care in the region. For years, residents had to rely on distant clinics and even travel abroad for advanced treatments. With the launch of Majan Eye Centre, that long-standing gap has been filled bringing world-class eye care closer to home.
